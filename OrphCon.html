"She knew the story of Orpheus, but it wasn't until the singing began that she realized it was the story of her life.(pg 124 SOW)"
Orfeo is referencing a version of the same story that was transformed into a play.
Something also notable is with the name change they also changed the ending to favor Orpheus.

Eurydice gets bitten and dies. Orpheus refused to let her go so he went to Hell to get her back from Hades.

Orpheus walked all the way to the entrance of Hell but, at the last moment he felt he could no longer trust Hades and decided to turn around.

He broke his deal with Hades and lost Euridice forever.
